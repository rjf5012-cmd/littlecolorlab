About Little Color Lab

Little Color Lab was created for busy teachers and parents who need clean, kid-friendly printable coloring pages without ads or pop-ups. Each coloring sheet is designed with bold outlines perfect for young artists in preschool, kindergarten, and early elementary school.

Our growing library features holiday coloring pages (Halloween, Christmas, Easter, Valentine’s Day), animal themes (dinosaurs, jungle animals, ocean animals), educational pages (alphabet letters, numbers, shapes), and fun favorites like unicorns, sports, space, dragons, trucks, and fairies.

New printable coloring pages are added frequently, giving you fresh worksheets, rainy-day activities, and classroom-ready coloring ideas year-round. Everything is free, easy to print, and 100% kid-safe.
